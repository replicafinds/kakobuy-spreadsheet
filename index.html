Tips

KakoBuy Haul Tips from the Community

  • Bundle orders to save on shipping. Shipping from KakoBuy is charged per package, not per item. A haul of 5 items ships for roughly the same cost as 1. Most experienced buyers do monthly hauls of 10–20 items.
  • Always request QC photos. The seller sends you photos of the actual product before it ships. If anything looks off — wrong colorway, bad stitching, incorrect tag — you can request a replacement before it leaves the warehouse.
  • Use the community for batch reviews. Before ordering anything, search the Telegram community for that product. Other buyers will have already posted QC photos and honest reviews.
  • Start with lower-risk items. For your first KakoBuy haul, start with hoodies and polos (under $20) before moving to sneakers. Lower price point, lower risk, and you get familiar with the process.
  • Check the spreadsheet for price updates. Seller prices on KakoBuy change regularly. The spreadsheet is updated daily to reflect current pricing — always verify before budgeting.
